COMPANY PROFILE

The DAVAO SECURITY & INVESTIGATION AGENCY, INC., (DASIA for brevity) was founded and established in September 8, 1972 under a sole proprietorship type of business. The nature and business of the Agency is to engage in Commercial Security Services both in government and private enterprises.

VISION

DAVAO SECURITY & INVESTIGATION AGENCY, INC., (DASIA for brevity) is commited to the vision of producing highly professional, efficient, dedicated, honest and reliable security guards, and envisions of becoming one of the nations supplier of motivated, competent and credible security personnel so that it would be ranked among the best in the Philippines in the security industry. We shall secure lives and other precious and valuable properties entrusted to us by our clientele.

MISSION

DAVAO SECURITY & INVESTIGATION AGENCY, INC., (DASIA for brevity) will preserve and maintain its long range objectives, that is, to become the country's best Private Security Agency producing and deploying security guards equipped with globally competitive and marketable knowledge and skills in security profession.

We shall strickly adhere to the value of dependability, which we will instill on each of our security guards, so that our individual clients would feel protected as regards their persons, as well as the properties that they entrust to our security personnel from dawn to dusk.
